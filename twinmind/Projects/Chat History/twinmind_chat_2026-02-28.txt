The escalation flow in the PO Tracker is designed to ensure timely follow-up and resolution of purchase order requests that are not approved or created within specified timeframes. Here's a summary of the escalation path:

1. **After 72 hours** – Escalate to the Property Manager (PM).
2. **After 96 hours** – Escalate to the Market Property Manager (MPM) and Market Technical Manager (MTM).
3. **After 120 hours** – Escalate to the Group Manager Market Operations (GMMO).
4. **After 144 hours** – Final escalation to GMMO.

If a purchase order (PO) does not appear in the system within the specified timeline, an escalation process is triggered to ensure accountability and prompt action.
